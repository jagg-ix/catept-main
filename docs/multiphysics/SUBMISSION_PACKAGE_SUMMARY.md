We present a framework extending quantum mechanics and general relativity 
to open systems through complex action χ = S_R + iℏτ_ent, where τ_ent is

G_μν + iΛ_μν = κ(T_μν + iS_μν) preserving general covariance. We prove

testable predictions: visibility decay V(S) = V_cl·exp(-λS) in 
epsilon-near-zero materials with geometric enhancement n_g ~ 10⁶, 
enabling tabletop tests without extreme conditions.
